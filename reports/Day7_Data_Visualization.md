# Day 7: Data Visualization

## Chart 1: Sales by Category

### Observation

Technology generated the highest sales among all categories.

### Business Insight

Technology is an important revenue-generating category and shows strong customer demand.

---

## Chart 2: Profit by Category

### Observation

Technology generated the highest profit among the categories.

### Business Insight

Technology is also an important contributor to overall profitability.

---

## Chart 3: Sales by Region

### Observation

The West region generated the highest sales.

### Business Insight

The company has strong sales performance in the West region and should continue focusing on this market.

---

## Chart 4: Sales by Sub-Category

### Observation

The phones sub-category generated the highest sales.

### Business Insight

This sub-category has strong customer demand and contributes significantly to revenue.

---

## Chart 5: Profit by Sub-Category

### Observation

The copiers sub-category generated the highest profit.

### Business Insight

This sub-category is an important contributor to the company's profitability.
